About Croissants

Croissants are a delicious French pastry known for their light, airy texture and rich, buttery flavor. They're made from layers of dough that are folded and baked to perfection. Whether you enjoy them plain or filled with chocolate, almond paste, or ham and cheese, croissants are a versatile treat suitable for any occasion.

Croissant Fun Facts
  • Croissants were originally inspired by a Viennese pastry called the kipferl.
  • The word "croissant" means "crescent" in French, which perfectly describes their shape.
  • To enjoy croissants at their best, warm them up in the oven for a few minutes.
  • While the classic plain croissant is a favorite, there are numerous variations, including almond croissants, chocolate croissants (pain au chocolat), and ham and cheese croissants.
  • Croissants and coffee make a classic and delicious combination. They are often enjoyed together, with the croissant's buttery goodness complementing the coffee's rich flavors.
